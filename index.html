Spontaneity

Have you ever wanted to plan a spontaneous meet-up with a group of your friends, be it a Chipotle run for dinner or a pick-up soccer game? Spontaneity allows you to push quickly created events to your friends in order to plan for a spontaneous meet up. Users simply create an account with our service, add users to different friend groups, and create events that they wish to happen within the day or add themselves to events that have been created by other people in their friend group.

Have an immediate urge to invite all your friends over to your dorm to watch a movie? Spontaneity can help you!
